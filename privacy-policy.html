Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

FSociety Computer Services ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding it.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when you:

  • Fill out our contact form (name, email address, message)
  • Book an appointment (name, email, phone number, service address, service type, preferred date and time, and any notes)

We do not use tracking cookies, analytics platforms, or third-party advertising scripts on this website.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiry or service request
  • To confirm, update, or cancel your booked appointment
  • To communicate about your service (reminders, follow-ups)

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Storage

Appointment data is stored securely in our booking database. Contact form submissions are sent directly to our business email and are not stored on a server beyond that transmission.

Email Communications

When you book an appointment, you will receive automated emails confirming your request and any status updates (confirmation or cancellation). These are transactional emails only — we do not send marketing newsletters.

Data Retention

We retain booking records for up to 12 months for business and warranty purposes. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for future communications
